Butter Chicken Pizza

Description

Butter Chicken Pizza is a fusion dish combining creamy butter chicken with a cheesy pizza base.

Butter Chicken Pizza – Ingredients & Recipe

Ingredients

  • Pizza base
  • Cooked chicken
  • Butter
  • Tomato gravy
  • Fresh cream
  • Pizza sauce
  • Onion, capsicum
  • Mozzarella cheese
  • Salt and spices

Recipe

Prepare butter chicken topping
Cook chicken with butter, tomato gravy, cream, and spices.
Prepare pizza base
Spread pizza sauce evenly on the base.
Add toppings
Add butter chicken mixture, vegetables, and cheese.
Bake
Bake at 200°C for 12–15 minutes until cheese melts.
