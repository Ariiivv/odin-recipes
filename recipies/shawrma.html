Shawarma

Description

Shawarma is a popular Middle Eastern dish made with spiced meat, wrapped in pita bread with vegetables and garlic sauce.

Ingredients

  • Chicken
  • Yogurt
  • Garlic
  • Lemon juice
  • Shawarma spices
  • Pita bread or roti
  • Onion, tomato, lettuce
  • Garlic sauce
  • Salt

Recipe

Marinate the chicken
Marinate chicken with yogurt, garlic, lemon juice, spices, and salt.
Cook the chicken
Grill or pan-fry the chicken until fully cooked.
Prepare the wrap
Warm pita or roti and spread garlic sauce.
Assemble
Add chicken and vegetables, roll tightly, and serve.
