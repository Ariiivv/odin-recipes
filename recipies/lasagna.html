Lasagna

Description

Lasagna is a traditional Italian dish made with layers of flat pasta sheets, rich meat or vegetable sauce, creamy béchamel, and melted cheese, baked until golden and flavorful.

Lasagna – Ingredients & Recipe

Ingredients

  • Lasagna sheets
  • Olive oil
  • Onion (chopped)
  • Garlic (chopped)
  • Tomato sauce or crushed tomatoes
  • Minced meat (chicken/beef) or mixed vegetables
  • Salt and pepper
  • Italian herbs (oregano, basil)
  • Butter
  • All-purpose flour
  • Milk
  • Cheese (mozzarella + parmesan)

Recipe

Prepare the sauce
Heat oil, sauté onion and garlic. Add minced meat or vegetables, then tomato sauce, salt, pepper, and herbs. Simmer for 10–15 minutes.
Make white sauce (béchamel)
Melt butter, add flour and cook briefly. Slowly add milk, stirring until thick. Add salt.
Cook pasta sheets
Boil lasagna sheets in salted water until just soft. Drain.
