Pizza

Description

Pizza is a popular Italian dish made with a flat bread base topped with sauce, cheese, and various vegetables or meats, baked until crispy.

Pizza – Ingredients & Recipe

Ingredients

  • Pizza base
  • Pizza sauce
  • Mozzarella cheese
  • Onion
  • Capsicum
  • Tomato
  • Olives (optional)
  • Salt and oregano

Recipe

Prepare the base
Place the pizza base on a tray and spread pizza sauce evenly.
Add toppings
Add vegetables and sprinkle cheese on top.
Bake
Bake at 200°C for 12–15 minutes until cheese melts.
Serve
Slice and serve hot.
